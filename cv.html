Professional experience

April 2008 - January 2010, I was working as a Java developer for Nebu Hungary Kft. in Debrecen. The company develops tools for custom marketing research using MSSQL, Java, AJAX and Delphi. Initially, I was implementing features and fixing bugs, but with time, my focus shifted to program and feature design.
February 2006 - February 2007, I was working as a part time software developer for the Positron Emission Tomography Centre of Debrecen. My job was to maintain and fix the current system as well as to design and implement statistical image reconstruction algorithms. In the one year spent at the position I have gained significant C/C++ Windows/Linux development experience.
December 2005 - January 2006, I participated in the translation of the book InDesign CS2 by Cohen Sandee for the Panem publisher. My part was to translate the 1st 4th 16th 18th and 21st chapters of the book from English to Hungarian. ISBN 963-54-5452-X
August 2002 - to date, I became a member of the Hungarian demo scene group called Ümlaüt Design. I usually participate in the development of tool-chain related software (C++).
